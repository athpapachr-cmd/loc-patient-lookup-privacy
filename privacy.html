Privacy Policy

The LOC Patient Lookup extension is used exclusively by Limassol Orthopaedic Center staff.

The extension reads a phone number entered in Cal.eu and sends it securely to the clinic backend for patient identification.

No data is sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising.

Data is used solely for appointment booking and patient identification purposes.

The extension stores the backend URL and API secret locally in the browser extension storage.
